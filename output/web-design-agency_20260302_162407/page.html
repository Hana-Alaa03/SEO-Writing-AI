While you can launch a site in any language, prioritizing an RTL-first design is a strategic necessity for engaging the local Arabic-speaking audience effectively. Your interface must feel natural to users who read from right to left, which involves more than just translating text; it requires mirroring layouts and navigation elements. You must ensure your digital infrastructure complies with local frameworks, such as those established by the National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA). Depending on your industry and the sensitivity of the data you collect, you may be required to host your information on servers located within Saudi Arabia. Adhering to these residency requirements protects your business from legal risks and ensures faster load times for your local visitors.
