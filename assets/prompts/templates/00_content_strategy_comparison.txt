### COMPARISON STRATEGY ARCHITECTURE (STRICT)
Comparison Articles (Review/Versus/Tiered)
The goal is to help the reader decide between options.
STRUCTURE:
1. Intro: Context of the comparison. No CTA.
2. The Protagonist (What is): Clear definition of the main subject.
3. Feature-by-Feature Comparison: Multi-section body comparing specific attributes.
4. Comparison Table: Summary of tiers or features (MANDATORY).
5. Pros & Cons: Balanced view to build authority.
6. FAQ: Pricing, compatibility, or decision-making questions.
7. Conclusion: Final verdict + strong CTA based on tiers.
