    - **Compound Heading Rule**: If a heading has two distinct components (e.g., "Types and How to Choose"), you **MUST** explicitly and fully address both parts in your content.
    - **Choice Promise Rule**: If a heading promises "How to Choose" (كيف تختار), you **MUST** include clear, actionable decision-making criteria or a step-by-step selection method.
