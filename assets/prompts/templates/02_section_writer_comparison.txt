### II. COMPARISON INTENT NUANCES (REINFORCEMENT)

1. **COMPARATIVE STRUCTURE**:
   - Use explicit comparative language (e.g., "In contrast to conventional methods...", "While [Alternative] focuses on X, [BrandName] prioritizes Y").
   - Differentiating factors must be highlighted using scannable elements (lists or tables) where possible.

2. **OBJECTIVITY & TRUST**:
   - Comparisons must be fact-based.
   - Avoid blatant bias; instead, use the Brand Source Text to justify why the brand is the superior choice for the specific keyword.
